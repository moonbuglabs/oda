The Four Contenders

Four names have been shortlisted. Each brings a different story and set of strategic implications to the table.

Lune

Derived from a geometric principle‚Äîa crescent shape formed by two circular arcs. Evokes precision, science, and a subtle connection to light (lunar).

Moda

From "mode" or "fashion," suggesting a new, modern method. Cleverly embeds "ODA" (Optical Design Automation) within the name.

Airy

A direct link to the Airy disk, a core optical physics concept. Leverages the legacy and reputation of our current firm, "Airy Optics."

ELE

From "element," signifying foundational components in optics and materials. Leverages existing company "ELE Optics, Inc." and domain ownership.
